Explore a melancholic island with brutalist structures where you'll ​encounter ​4 tormented characters. ​Gradually understand their desires trough a surprising puzzle/dating sim gameplay by caressing or holding the characters. ​Will they trust you enough to ask you one last thing?

Player Non Player is a queer game created by Jonathan Coryn that explores mature themes, such as grief, through the lens of intimacy, including sexual and gender identity, friendship and love. ​Released alongside Agar Agar's new album of the same name, the video game shares a unified lore, with the music intricately tied to the game's narrative. Notably, the Trouble music clip serves as a prequel to the events depicted in the game.
